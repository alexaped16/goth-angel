About Me

My name is Ellie and I live in Chicago. I recently earned a Master's Degree from The Chicago School of Professional Psychology, and I am now a Clinical Mental Health Counselor. I have always loved art. My desire to create art on a more consistent basis grew after taking drawing classes in Florence and Paris. I started Goth Angel as a passion project after returning from Paris. Making jewelry is my creative outlet, and am so thankful for every purchase made. Every piece is made with love!
